Zhou Group: Fault-Tolerant Quantum Architectures

Welcome to the Zhou group! We are a research group at MIT led by Hengyun (Harry) Zhou, developing full-stack architectures for resource-efficient, fault-tolerant quantum computing. Our work spans quantum error correction, algorithms and compilation, and hardware architecture. We work closely with other theory and experimental groups at MIT and beyond.
